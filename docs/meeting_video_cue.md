# NotebookLM video — cue sheet

Your ~7 minute NotebookLM video carries the vision language. Live demo proves it.

**You own the video file** (not in this repo). Cue it before the meeting.

---

## Tonight checklist

- [ ] Locate the NotebookLM export (MP4 / share link)
- [ ] Trim or skip intros so runtime is **≤7 minutes** (agenda slot is ~5–12)
- [ ] Save an **offline copy** on the laptop Desktop (Wi‑Fi can flake)
- [ ] Open the player, scrub to 0:00, leave paused full-screen ready
- [ ] Confirm audio works on the meeting room / Zoom speakers

---

## When to play

After the one-line open (*raw export ≠ deliverable*), before the live click-through.
See [`meeting_demo_script.md`](meeting_demo_script.md).
